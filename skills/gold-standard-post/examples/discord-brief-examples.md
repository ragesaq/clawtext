## Notes on Discord Brief Style

**What works:**
- **Emoji first** — Users see it in the feed immediately
- **Bold product name** — Stands out even without headers
- **Problem in 1 sentence** — Make them feel it
- **Solution in 1 sentence** — Make it clear
- **Link at the end** — No need for verbose CTA
- **Under 50 words** — Scannable in one glance

**What doesn't work:**
- Walls of text (Discord is a feed scanner tool)
- Multiple links (confusing, use one main link)
- Headers or tables (doesn't render well)
- More than 2 emoji (noise)
- No context about why they should care

**Posting strategy:**
- Post to #ai-projects or relevant product channel
- If it's important, thread it below with more detail (README, QUICKSTART)
- Use Discord Brief for the announcement; link to full docs for deep dives
