# Wordcraft Skill

**Purpose:** Deep language optimization, retextualization, and wordcrafting assistance

## How It Works

Drop any text here for optimization. I'll help you:

- **Refine tone and voice** - Make it sharper, warmer, more professional, or whatever fits
- **Restructure for impact** - Reorganize ideas for better flow and clarity
- **Elevate word choice** - Find the perfect words, not just the right words
- **Adjust register** - Shift between casual, formal, technical, poetic, etc.
- **Condense or expand** - Tighten prose or add depth where needed
- **Polish rough drafts** - Turn thoughts into finished pieces
- **Retextualize concepts** - Express the same idea in fundamentally different ways

## How to Use

Just drop your text with any context you have:

```
"Here's what I'm thinking: [your text]
Want it to sound more [X] / less [Y] / fit [audience]"
```

Or just:
```
"Optimize this: [your text]"
```

No formal structure needed. Random drop-ins are exactly what this is for.

## What I Bring

- **Precision editing** - Every word earns its place
- **Stylistic range** - From technical to poetic to conversational
- **Structural thinking** - How ideas connect and build
- **Tone awareness** - Knowing when formality helps and when it hurts
- **Voice preservation** - Enhancing your voice, not replacing it

## Examples of What Works Well

- Discord messages you want to land better
- Documentation that needs clarity
- Creative writing that needs polish
- Technical explanations that need simplification
- Persuasive text that needs more punch
- Anything where the *way* you say it matters as much as *what* you say
