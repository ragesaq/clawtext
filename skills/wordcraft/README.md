# Wordcraft — Language Optimization Skill

## Overview

This skill provides deep language optimization and retextualization services. Think of it as having a skilled editor, wordsmith, and communication strategist available on demand.

## Capabilities

### 1. Tone & Voice Adjustment
- Shift between formal, casual, professional, friendly, authoritative, humble, etc.
- Match audience expectations
- Preserve or transform your natural voice

### 2. Structural Optimization
- Reorganize for logical flow
- Improve readability and comprehension
- Build better arguments and narratives

### 3. Word Choice Enhancement
- Find more precise, evocative, or appropriate terms
- Eliminate redundancy and weak language
- Elevate without pretension

### 4. Register Shifting
- Technical → Accessible
- Casual → Professional
- Dense → Clear
- Simple → Sophisticated

### 5. Retextualization
- Express the same core idea in fundamentally different ways
- Find fresh angles on familiar concepts
- Adapt content for different contexts/audiences

### 6. Polishing
- Grammar and mechanics (obvious, but included)
- Rhythm and cadence
- Clarity and concision
- Impact and memorability

## Use Cases

- **Discord messages** - Make them land better, avoid misunderstandings
- **Documentation** - Clarity, precision, usability
- **Creative writing** - Prose refinement, voice development
- **Technical communication** - Complex ideas made accessible
- **Persuasive writing** - Arguments, pitches, proposals
- **Emails and professional comms** - Tone, clarity, effectiveness
- **Any text where word choice matters**

## How to Engage

Just drop your text. Add context if you have it:

**Minimal:**
```
"Optimize this: [text]"
```

**With direction:**
```
"Make this sound more [X]"
"Less [Y], more [Z]"
"Target audience: [who]"
"Goal: [what you want it to do]"
```

**Open-ended:**
```
"[text] — what do you think?"
```

## Philosophy

- **Your voice, amplified** - I enhance, don't replace
- **Precision over flourish** - Every word should earn its place
- **Context matters** - The same words work differently in different settings
- **Iterative is fine** - We can refine together
- **No judgment** - Rough drafts are where good writing starts

## Limitations

- I work with text, not audio/video
- I can't access external style guides unless you provide them
- For highly specialized domains (legal, medical), I'll flag where expert review is needed
